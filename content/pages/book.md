# Book an Event

Path A — instant self-serve booking via Wix Bookings. Packages:
[`content/packages/packages.json`](../packages/packages.json). Setup:
[runbook 03](../../docs/runbooks/03-wix-bookings-setup.md).

## Intro
Have a standard event? Book it in minutes. Pick a package, choose your date, and reserve with a
secure deposit (processed via Square). We'll confirm by email and follow up on the details.

## Not sure which package? *(guest-count helper)*
Enter your guest count and we'll point you to the right package — or, for larger events, to a
custom quote. *(Powered by [`velo/pages/book.page.js`](../../velo/pages/book.page.js).)*

## What happens next
1. You reserve your date with a deposit (via Square).
2. You get an instant confirmation + receipt by email.
3. We reach out to finalize menu, timing, and setup details.
